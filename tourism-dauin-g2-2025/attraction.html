Top Attractions in Dauin

Apo Island

Famous for snorkeling and diving, Apo Island is a marine sanctuary with vibrant coral reefs and sea turtles.

Dauin White Beach

Relax on pristine white sands and enjoy breathtaking sunsets at the peaceful Dauin White Beach.

Lake Balinsasayao

A scenic lake surrounded by forested hills, perfect for kayaking, boating, and nature photography.

Tubod Falls

A refreshing waterfall hidden in lush greenery, ideal for swimming and a peaceful getaway from the city.
