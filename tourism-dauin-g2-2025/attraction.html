Top Attractions in Dauin

Mag-aso steam cave

Mag-Aso Cave is a small natural cave in Dauin with cool spring water and scenic rock formations, offering a peaceful nature spot.

Baslay Highland Brew Coffee

Baslay Highland Brew Coffee is a scenic mountain café in Dauin known for its locally grown coffee, peaceful atmosphere, and stunning highland views.

Dauin Man-made Forest

The Dauin Man-Made Forest is a peaceful, tree-lined area offering cool shade, scenic views, and a refreshing nature escape.

Salaya Beach Houses

Salaya Beach Houses is a luxury beachfront resort in Dauin known for its elegant suites, peaceful atmosphere, and easy access to top diving spots.

St. Nicolas of Tolentino Church

St. Nicolas of Tolentino Church is a historic Dauin landmark known for its traditional architecture, serene atmosphere, and cultural significance to the local community.

Dauin Beach

Dauin’s beaches are known for their dark volcanic sand, clear waters, and excellent access to world-class diving and snorkeling spots.
