About the Town

Dauin is a peaceful coastal town in Negros Oriental known for its world-class diving, beautiful beaches, and thriving marine life. As the gateway to the famous Apo Island, it attracts divers and nature lovers from all over the world with its crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and frequent sea turtle sightings.

Beyond the underwater scenery, Dauin is a town with a warm and welcoming spirit. Life moves at a relaxed coastal pace, with locals known for their hospitality and deep connection to the sea. Coconut-lined roads, quiet rural communities, and charming seaside villages create a peaceful atmosphere that instantly makes visitors feel at home.

The town also offers meaningful eco-experiences—such as reef conservation programs where visitors can help build artificial coral reefs and support ongoing marine restoration efforts. Calm beaches, stunning sunrises, and scenic coastal walks add to Dauin’s natural beauty.

Whether you're exploring the marine sanctuary, diving around Apo Island, or simply enjoying the slow life by the sea, Dauin offers a perfect blend of adventure, culture, and serene island living.
