About Dauin

Dauin is a beautiful coastal town in Negros Oriental, known for its stunning marine biodiversity and beaches.

It is the gateway to Apo Island, a world-famous diving destination, attracting tourists from all over the globe.

Dauin is a blend of culture, nature, and adventure, offering activities from diving to cultural experiences and local cuisine.
